# Logos

The NorCal SBDC logo is the primary visual identifier for our brand. Consistent usage builds recognition and trust.

---

## Primary Logo

The primary logo features the full "NorCal SBDC" wordmark. Use this whenever space permits and the brand needs full identification.

## Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "S" in SBDC around all sides of the logo. No other elements should intrude into this zone.

## Minimum Size

The logo should never appear smaller than **120px wide** on digital applications or **1 inch wide** in print.

---

## Usage Guidelines

- **Do** use the white logo on dark backgrounds (navy, navy-deep)
- **Do** use the dark logo on light backgrounds (cream, white)
- **Don't** stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- **Don't** change the logo colors outside of the approved palette
- **Don't** add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
- **Don't** place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a color overlay
