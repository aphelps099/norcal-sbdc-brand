### Logo Guidelines
- **Clear Space**: Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "S" character
- **Minimum Size**: 1.25 inches width (print) / 90 pixels width (digital)
- **Co-branding**: Partner logos positioned to the immediate right with appropriate spacing

## DESIGN LANGUAGE SUMMARY

### Color Palette
| Use | Color | Hex |
|-----|-------|-----|
| Primary Brand | Navy Blue | `#012D61` |
| Accent/CTA | Strawberry Red | `#F7024D` |
| Secondary | Sky Blue | `#40C4FF` |
| Dark Text | Dark Grey | `#2A2D34` |
| Borders/Dividers | Steel Grey | `#CFD2D8` |
| Backgrounds | Light Grey | `#E0E0E0` |

### Typography
- **Primary**: Helvetica Neue (ultra-light 100 through regular 400)
- **Technical/Code**: Roboto Mono
- Style: Clean, Swiss/corporate modernism — all-caps tracking for labels and section tags

### Imagery Style
- **Business/Community photos**: ribbon-cuttings, entrepreneurs, diverse business owners
- **Editorial stock**: lifestyle photography (person on phone, nature, products)
- **Full-width, high-quality photography**
- Mix of dark dramatic and warm editorial tones

### Layout Patterns
- **Homepage**: Full-screen centered, minimal, one-pager
- **Brand guide pages**: Left sidebar (page outline nav) + right main content
- **Platform page**: Scrolling sections with sticky navigation
- **Typography**: Large display headings, tracked uppercase section labels (in red/accent)
- **Section labels**: "SECTION 001", "SECTION 002" in Roboto Mono accent color

### Design Tone
- **Professional but approachable**
- Clean Swiss/International Style typography
- Dark navy backgrounds for platform sections
- White space-heavy content areas
- Red/salmon accents for CTAs and section labels
- Bold weight transitions for typographic hierarchy
